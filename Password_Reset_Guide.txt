
TECHNOVA SOLUTIONS
PASSWORD RESET GUIDE

Version: 1.0

====================================================

1. PURPOSE

This guide explains how employees can securely reset their company password.

====================================================

2. WHEN SHOULD YOU RESET YOUR PASSWORD?

• Forgotten password
• Password expired after 90 days
• Suspicious account activity
• IT security recommendation

====================================================

3. PASSWORD RESET STEPS

Step 1:
Visit https://password.technova.local

Step 2:
Click "Forgot Password"

Step 3:
Enter your company email.

Step 4:
Verify your identity using MFA.

Step 5:
Create a new password.

====================================================

4. PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS

• Minimum 8 characters
• One uppercase letter
• One lowercase letter
• One number
• One special character
• Cannot reuse previous 5 passwords

====================================================

5. ACCOUNT LOCKED

If your account is locked:

• Wait 15 minutes

OR

• Contact IT Support

====================================================

6. MFA ISSUES

If MFA is not working:

• Restart Authenticator App

• Check mobile date and time

• Re-register MFA device

====================================================

7. SECURITY TIPS

• Never share passwords.

• Never write passwords on paper.

• Use unique passwords.

====================================================

8. CONTACT IT

itsupport@technova.com

Monday-Friday

9 AM - 6 PM
