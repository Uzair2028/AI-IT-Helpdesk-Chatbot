
TECHNOVA SOLUTIONS
VPN USER GUIDE

Version: 1.0

====================================================

1. PURPOSE

The Virtual Private Network (VPN) allows employees to securely access company resources while working remotely.

====================================================

2. SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

• Windows 10 or later
• macOS 12 or later
• Stable internet connection
• Company VPN Client installed

====================================================

3. HOW TO CONNECT

Step 1:
Open the TechNova VPN Client.

Step 2:
Enter your company email address.

Step 3:
Enter your password.

Step 4:
Complete Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).

Step 5:
Click the Connect button.

====================================================

4. COMMON VPN ERRORS

Error:
Authentication Failed

Possible Causes:
• Incorrect password
• Expired password
• Incorrect email address

Solution:
Reset your password and try again.

----------------------------------------------------

Error:
Unable to Reach VPN Server

Possible Causes:
• Internet connection unavailable
• Company VPN servers under maintenance

Solution:
Check internet connection and try again after 10 minutes.

----------------------------------------------------

Error:
Connection Timed Out

Solution:
Restart the VPN client and reconnect.

====================================================

5. SECURITY RULES

• Never share VPN credentials.
• Always disconnect after work.
• Do not use public Wi-Fi without VPN.
• Report suspicious login activity immediately.

====================================================

6. TROUBLESHOOTING

Problem:
VPN keeps disconnecting.

Possible Solution:
• Restart your computer.
• Restart your router.
• Update VPN client.
• Check firewall settings.

----------------------------------------------------

Problem:
MFA code not received.

Possible Solution:
• Check Authenticator App.
• Verify phone date and time.
• Contact IT Support.
